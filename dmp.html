Ampewbound DMP Space Race Server Guidelines

Failure to follow these guidelines will result in severe penalization

Failure to read the rules or read them properly is the player's fault

  1. No controlling others’ ships without their explicit permission
  2. No taking or using others’ stuff (vehicles, parts, ect.)
  3. No sabotaging others’ crafts (yet)
  4. No intentional glitching or exploiting
  5. No attempting to land on or get near the sun
  6. No whining
  7. Absolutely no loopholes (def. 3)

While we say ‘space race’ it doesn’t really actually matter. Just play the game.
Achievements will be logged here, along with who made them.
