Server Guidelines

Failure to follow these rules will result in a kick or ban

Failure to read the rules properly is the player’s fault

Suggestions welcome

  • Teleportation

    • You are only allowed to teleport to another player or a player’s base with their direct permission

    • You are not allowed to teleport to or anywhere near (250 blocks) another’s player or base in order to attack them

      • Nor are you allowed to teleport ‘outside of their base’

  • You are not allowed to kill yourself on purpose for any reason including…

    • Regenerating hunger

    • Regenerating health

    • Acquiring blood

    • “Just to see what happens”

  • A player does not necessarily have to let their opponent know of their ‘declaration of war’

  • Raiding and other destruction of another player’s property is only allowed whilst they are online or have not been online for 2 hours

    • This is to disallow a player to raid another shortly after they log off

  • All treaties must be honored

  • No treaty can last forever, there must be a deadline that is within a reasonable timespan

  • The mod ICBM is not to be used at this time, as it is unstable and could crash the server

  • In the map and minimap (if enabled), players must have the following turned off…

    • Other players

    • Animals

    • Pets

    • Enemy Mobs

  • Absolutely no loopholes (def. 3)
