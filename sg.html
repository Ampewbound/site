Server Guidelines

Any infringements will result in a kick or ban

Suggestions welcome

  • Teleportation

    • You are only allowed to teleport to another player or a player’s base with their direct permission

    • You are not allowed to teleport to or anywhere near (~200-300 blocks) another player or a player’s base in order to attack them

      • Nor are you allowed to teleport ‘outside of their base’

  • You are not allowed to kill yourself on purpose for any reason including…

    • Regenerating hunger

    • Regenerating health

    • Acquiring blood

    • “Just to see what happens”

  • A player does not necessarily have to let their opponent know of their ‘declaration of war’

  • Any and all raids and other destruction of another players property is allowed

  • The mod ICBM is not to be used at this time, as it is unstable and could crash the server

  • In the minimap, players must not have the following turned on…

    • Other players

    • Animals

    • Pets

    • Enemy Mobs

  • Absolutely no loopholes (def. 3)
