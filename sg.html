Ampewbound Forge Server Guidelines

Failure to follow these guidelines will result in a kick or ban

Failure to read the geuidelines or read them properly is the player's fault

  1. Teleportation
    1. You are only allowed to teleport to another player or a player's base with their direct permission
    2. You are not allowed to teleport to or anywhere near (250 blocks) another player's base in order to attack them
  2. You are not allowed to purposely kill yourself for any reason including...
    1. Regenerating hunger
    2. Regenerating health
    3. Acquiring blood
    4. "Just to see what happens"
  3. A player does not necessarily have to let their opponent know of their 'declaration of war'
  4. Raiding and other destruction of another player's property is only allowed whilst they are online or have not been online for 1 hour
    • This is to disallow a player to raid another shortly after they log off
  5. Treaties
    1. All treaties must be honored
    2. No treaty can last forever, there must be a deadline that is within a reasonable timespan
    3. And no, there is not "a treaty that end all other treaties"
    4. Nor is there a treaty that "allows the users to not follow the guidelines"
    5. I could go on all day
  6. The mod ICBM is not to be used at this time, as it is unstable and could crash the server
  7. In the map and minimap (if enabled), players must have the following turned off...
    1. Other Players
    2. Mobs
    3. Pets
    4. Enemy Mobs
  8. Absolutely no loopholes (def. 3)
